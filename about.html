About Us: Nebula Quest Entertainment

Founded on November 11, 2023,

Nebula Quest Entertainment is a dynamic indie game development company,

driven by the creative prowess of its founders, Yarno Roethof and Quidon Roethof.

With Yarno specializing in programming

and Quidon wearing the hats of both programmer and artist,

the duo forms a formidable team dedicated to crafting engaging

and entertaining games for a diverse audience.

Our Mission: Elevating Fun for Everyone

At Nebula Quest Entertainment, we are committed to breaking the mold of traditional game development.

Our mission is to deliver games that transcend boundaries and captivate players from all walks of life.

By blending technical expertise with artistic innovation, we strive to create immersive experiences that resonate with a global audience.

Join us on our journey as we embark on the exciting adventure of game development, setting the stage for a new era of interactive entertainment. Nebula Quest Entertainment - Where Fun Knows No Limits!
